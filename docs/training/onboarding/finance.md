## Screens you'll use (day one)

1. **Finance → Invoices** — AR from shipments
2. **Finance → Bills** — AP from procurement
3. **Finance → Accounts** — chart of accounts
4. **Finance → Reports** — P&L and balance sheet

## Top tasks

1. Review auto-posted invoices from sales shipments.
2. Record customer payments against open AR.
3. Post vendor bills and record AP payments.
4. Run monthly P&L and balance sheet reports.
5. Verify credit memos from Returns resolutions.
